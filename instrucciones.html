HOW TO PLAY

- Press PLAY to begin the game.


- Press CHOOSE to sacrifice a coder, but they will be chosen by the game randomly.


- Press the killing method you prefer to sacrifice the chosen coder: STAB, ELECTROCUTE or SHOOT.


- Press the DEATHLIST to add "+" or eliminate "-" names from the list. But once a coder has been sacrificed, there is no turning back!


- The remaining coder still alive will be the winner! But if you choose to play again, they may not be so lucky!


Let the Frankenwheel of Doom begin!
